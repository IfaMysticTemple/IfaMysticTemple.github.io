Initiations

We offer a transformative journey designed for individuals who are ready to step into a deeper sense of identity, purpose, and personal power. This program honors the universal human need for guided transition—whether from adolescence into adulthood, adulthood into leadership, or from one life chapter into the next. Rooted in community, reflection, and experiential learning, the program blends traditional rite‑of‑passage principles with modern personal‑development practices. Participants move through a structured series of stages that include self‑discovery, skill‑building, challenge, mentorship, and integration. Each stage is crafted to help individuals release outdated patterns, claim their strengths, and embrace the responsibilities and opportunities of their next phase of life.

Ifa Classes

Whether you are looking to build a personal practice or deepen your path as a devotee, these Ifa & Orisa Divination Classes provide a structured, hands-on foundation in the sacred art of communication with the Divine. This curriculum bridges the gap between theory and practice, teaching you how to "open the mouth" of the oracles to receive clear, actionable guidance.

What You Will Learn:
Consecration & Care: How to properly birth, feed, and store your divination tools.
The Ethics of Inquiry: How to ask the right questions and maintain the integrity of the oracle.
Invocation (Oriki): Learning the specific prayers and chants used to open a session and invite the presence of the deities.
Interpreting the Fall: Moving beyond rote memorization to intuitive and systematic interpretation.

Note: These classes are designed to respect traditional lineages. While some levels are open to beginners, certain advanced aspects of Merindinlogun may require specific initiatory standing.
