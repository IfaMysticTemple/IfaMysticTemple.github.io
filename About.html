Our mission

The mission of the Ifa Mystic Temple of Osa Otura, the Door of Truth, is to guide individuals toward the realization of their innate gifts, divine purpose, and spiritual inheritance through self-knowledge and alignment with universal truth.

We are committed to creating a sacred space where those who are ready can examine and release inherited psychological conditioning, reclaim their authentic identity, and consciously walk a path of spiritual liberation. The Temple welcomes all who are willing to do the inner work required to awaken, grow, and live in harmony with their highest truth.
